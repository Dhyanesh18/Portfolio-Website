About Me

Hi, I'm a 3rd-year Computer Science student at IIIT Sri City with a deep interest in Artificial Intelligence. I love working with Python and its powerful libraries for data analysis, machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and natural language processing.

Over time, I've grown especially passionate about Natural Language Processing (NLP), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Reinforcement Learning (RL). Right now, I'm exploring how LLMs can be integrated into real-world applications, while also learning more about web development, model integration, and deployment.

Beyond academics, I'm actively involved in student communities. I was Core Member for AIML & Data Science at Google Developer Groups IIIT Sri City (2024-25) and the current Co-Lead of NexSync (2025-26), where I get to collaborate, build impactful projects, and share knowledge in the AI/ML space.
